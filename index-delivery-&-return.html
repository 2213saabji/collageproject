Delivery & Returns

shop-now.in committed to deliver products in excellent condition and fastest possible time. Delivery time may vary on location accessibility but standard duration will be 4-5 working days even some adverse condition may add 2 days extra.

To ensure your order receive on time, please make no mistakes filling your address details as follow: Complete address details with House Number, Floor, Street Name and Locality along with a Landmark. Enter correct City, State and Zip/Postal Code details. Kindly provide serviceable mobile number as primary contact preference and appreciated if alternative number is provided to resolve failed primary mobile number. We may require to call for delivery related queries. Important note to remember during Cash on Delivery orders, please make sure that someone should be available at the shipping address to pay cash by amount-tendering exact change.

If you receive a damaged parcel, please DO NOT accept it and email us at info@shop-now.in. Note: We make sure that all the parcels are perfectly packaged in box-wrapped with security tapes when dispatched from order-processing unit. Parcels go through courier service for the delivery and may some-times arrive in damaged state. We advise you to not accept any damaged/deformed parcels, in this particular case it will be your responsibility if product is missing or damaged.

If you want to return a delivered order, shipping charges will not be refunded. However, if you self-ship your return-order, consideration may entertain on reimburse of self-shipment charges solely based on Returns Policy. In the accounts, whose return condition of item violate our fair usage policy, Rs.60/- will be charge on all orders irrespective of order value.

All the purchases of ₹600* or greater will be free of charge door-step delivery and delivery/shipping charge of Rs.60/- will be applicable for all the orders under Rs.600/-. Each order-placed can be shipped only to single shipping address. If you wish to ship products to different addresses, you have to place multiple orders. *Order value is calculated after applicable service charges/discounts/VAT/GST.

Shop-now.in fair policy, we are committed to offer best customer experience. However, we notice our liberal easy-returns are abused in some accounts. These accounts typically return most of the items purchased or refuse our shipments. Hence, rest of the customers are deprived of opportunity to purchase these items. To safe guard the rights of our customers, we reserve the right to charge shipping cost of Rs.60/- for all orders and disable cash on delivery option for accounts which has high percentage of returns and shipments not accepted by value of orders placed.

Cancellation and Modifications

What is Shop-Now.In Cancellation Policy?

We strive to fulfill every order. However, certain orders are compelled to cancel due to unforeseen circumstances. The reasons could be product availability, inaccurate address, product errors, pricing and stock information error, or problems detected by our credit and fraud avoidance department.

If your card is debited after your order placed has cancel from our side, the exact amount will be credit to your account on same payment mode on request or you can avail it for store credit to buy other options at the same time or later.

Order cancelling can be entertained before the package dispatch from warehouse only. However, you will have the option to opt for store credits which you can use to buy something else at the same time or at a later time.

Is there any option for address change after the order placed?

Yes, you can change the shipping address of your order before the dispatch from store, by informing via email:info@shop-now.in.

How to I cancel an Order?

To cancel an order, again write to us an email: info@shop-now.in with your Order Number.

When will process the refund amount?

Cash on delivery mode has no refund since you haven’t paid for the order as it is free delivery. For payments made via Credit Card, Debit Card, Net Banking, or e-Wallet will credit into the source account within 7-10 working days from cancellation date.
